Why Worship With Us

We are a community of dedicated believers; committed to loving God, loving people, making disciples and impacting the world

A Welcoming Church

At TCI, it is our culture not just to welcome you warmly, but to ensure you feel at home and encourage you to be a part of the house. We believe that a beating path to our doorstep is a pointer to a divine direction given you to locate a new home and become part of our larger loving family.

Find Out More

A Guest Friendly Environment

It is not just a slogan but a believe for us that, “you can only be a guest once, in our midst, thereafter you a part of the family.” This is because you are in your father’s house. Therefore, our environment is structured in such a way that you can simply find your way around without a ‘tour guide’. Though, we are a developing family; with some facilities under construction, we do not take for granted the need for you to locate your destination easily and promptly within our vicinity.

Find Out More

A Worship Encouraging Ambience

Our first line of dedication is to God, whom we owe wholesome worship. To encourage this, TCI has put in, all it requires to create an ambience for true worship. Every of our service, from take off to touch down, is always power-packed and anointing soaked.

Find Out More

A Child-Friendly System

We understand the need for your wards to be properly taken care of while you enjoy an impacting worship experience in fellowship with other believers. For this reason, our Impact Children Ministry, filled with capable, experienced and godly men and women; are serving the Lord with dedication, to ensure that your children also enjoy a great time of worship and have an encouraging atmosphere for fellowship during our services.

Find Out More

A Teenage Impacting Ministry

With a Teenage Ministry equipped with dedicated and experienced hands, we also cater for the need of our teenagers, treating them with all specialty, that their stage in life requires.

Find Out More

A Youth Driven Congregation

A foundation laid and a structure being built upon, through the sweat of godly dedicated young men and women, TCI is a congregation whose activities is being facilitated by young minds bristling and bustling with dynamic Holy Spirit inspired ideas and strategies.

Maximum Impact Sunday Service
Pastor Abiodun Aribike May, 2021

We gather every Sunday morning, under an undiluted Holy Spirit filled atmosphere to worship, for spiritual warfare, biblically sound teachings, heavenly generated impartations and divinely directed instructions.

Maximum Impact Mid-week Service
Pastor Abiodun Aribike May, 2021

TCI is originally referenced as a praying church. We have over the years kept faith with this tradition, which is an effective platform for a functional ministry. In doing this, our mid-week service is solely dedicated to prayers, there we engage in spiritual warfare, connecting heaven to earth and delivering tangible results in the process.
