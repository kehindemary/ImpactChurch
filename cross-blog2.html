Events At The Cross

At the cross many things happened, when Jesus died and crucified at the cross, many things happened out of which are

There was forgiveness of sins for all of us: the sins of the past, present and future, Jesus made a provision for the forgiveness of such sins. Forgiveness is setting aside a personal hurt or injury. Jesus set aside all the insult, hurt and injury we caused Him;

In Him we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of our trespasses according to the riches of His grace.

Ephesians 1:7.
Who is this forgiveness for?

To Him all the prophets bear witness that everyone who believes in Him receives forgiveness of sins through His name.

Acts 10: 43.
It is for all who believe in Jesus, who confesses Him as Lord and believe Him in their heart.

I am He who brought out your transgression for my own sake, and I will not remember your sins anymore.

Isaiah 43:21.
When we confess our sins, and believes in Him, He forgives and blot out all our sins and transgressions.

As far as the east is from the west, so far does He remove our sins and transgressions from us.

Psalm 103:12

He paid the price for our redemption: To redeem is to buy something back, something that has been sold to slavery, something sold to another, is to buy the freedom back something in captivity. Sin has made us captive to evil habit, to the devil, to hell and death but Jesus came and purchase us back from this captivity.

For the wages of sin is death but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus

Romans 6:23
but in
1 Corithians 6:20

you have been bought with a price, so glorify God in your body

When we were still in our sins, iniquities, unrighteousness, and are sold out to the captivity, Jesus came and brought us back. What is the price of which we have been bought

In Him we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of our trespasses according to the riches of His grace

Ephesians 1:7
in Him we have been bought back and the price is the Blood of Jesus.

and they sang a new song saying, worthy are you to take the scroll and to open seal for you were slain and by your blood you ransom people from every tribe and language and people and nation

Revelation 5:9

He paid the ransom: Sin and devil kidnapped us, Jesus came to pay the price, He paid the ransom so that we can be free from the nature and power of sins, so that we can be free from the hands of the enemy and the devil.

for there is one God, and there is one mediator between God and men, he man Jesus Christ who gave Himself as a ransom for all which is a testimony given at the proper time

1 Timothy 2:5-6

Jesus paid the ransom with His life willingly for all, He paid price and redeemed us back. So, you do not need to continue in your present state, you need to believe in Jesus Christ and confess Him with your mouth and you will be ransom from sin, from death, hell and from everything that the devil is using against you. That is the purpose of the cross for you and I. Therefore, I pray for you, anyone in captivity of sin, death and the devil be release by the finished work of Christ in the name of Jesus

He delivered us: He delivered us from this present world, he delivered us from this present age, from the current evil in the world.

Grace to you and peace from God our father and the Lord Jesus Christ, who gave Himself for our sins to deliver us from the present evil age according to the will of God our father

Galatians 1:3-4

One of the things that Jesus went to the cross for and shed His blood for is to deliver us from this present evil age. There is no doubt about it that this present world is evil, you need only to listen to news on radio and television likewise social media to know that this present age is evil of which the death, burial and resurrection of Jesus Christ delivered us from it all.

we know that we are from God and the whole world lies in wickedness

1 John 5:19

The whole world lies under the power of the wicked one. I pray for you, every evil wickedness you are going through in this present age, be deliver from them in the name of Jesus. Jesus knows that we are in the world full of wickedness and that is why He came to deliver us, therefor any evil one saying No to your advancement, let an end come upon them in the name of Jesus.

And even if our gospel is veil, it is veil to those who are perishing, in their case, the god of this world have blinded the minds of unbelievers to keep them from seeing the light of the glorious gospel of Jesus, who is the image of God

2 Corithians 4:3-4

One thing the devil is doing in this age is to blindfold us to the good news of Christ, to veil our mind for us not to understand the things of the gospel. I pray for you, any covering over your mind, soul, spirit, eyes, ears, preventing you from understanding the gospel of Jesus, gospel of healing, of deliverance let the veil be taken away now in the name of Jesus. This present age is a world of hatred, a world of veil.

If the world hates you, know that it has hated me before it hated you If you were of the world, the world will love you as its own but because you are not of the world, but I choose you out of the world, therefore the world hates you

John 15:8-19

Are you going through hatred of the world? Are your bosses, family members, friend, neighbors against you, without any reason of you? Hear the word of the Lord, let the Lord take away every hatred and any punishment and any disappointment from this hatred from your life in the name of Jesus.

Conclusion

Amid the hatred of this world, I am here to you that Jesus loves you and that is why He went to the cross of calvary to die for your sins, to take away the works of darkness over your life. All you need to do is to surrender your life to Jesus Christ. He was hated by the world, yet He still prevails and today He is the King of kings and the Lord of lords and whatever you are going through now in this present age, He went to the cross to give you power to overcome them. Therefore, come to Him, the one that have the solution to all life problems, and He brought the solution by His death, burial, and resurrection.
